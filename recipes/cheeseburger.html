CheeseBurgeer

Description

The cheeseburger is a very popular meal served all around the globe. It is very easy to make!

Ingredients

  • BUrger's bread
  • beef steak
  • tomato and salad
  • cheese
  • garlic
  • salt
  • Mayo

Steps

  1. Start by cooking the beef on the stove, try to give the steak a circular form
  2. When the meat is ready the only thing left to do is to assemble the burger
  3. Bottom bread
  4. Then a layer of Mayo
  5. Then salad and tomatoes
  6. Then meat
  7. Then hot melting cheese
  8. Then another salad then top bread and it's readyyy!
