Pasta!

Description:

The Pasta is a simple and delicious meal easy to make. just made of pasta and meat sauce

Ingredients

  • pasta
  • meat balls
  • tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Start by cooking the sauce with tomato sauce
  2. Add some onions and garlic for the taste
  3. Add the meat balls in the sauce
  4. Cook the pasta in boiling water
  5. When the pasta is ready put it in a recipe and add the meat sauce on top
  6. You can add some cheese on top too
  7. Ready to be served!
