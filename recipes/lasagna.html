LASAGNA!

Description

The lasagna is a delicious meal made of cheese and meat . It is Garfield's favorite food!

Ingredients

  • lasagna sheet
  • beef
  • tomato
  • cheese
  • garlic
  • salt

Steps

  1. Put the beef and all the spices on the stove
  2. Add tomatoes and some garlic
  3. Check if there is enoough salt and if not add some
  4. When the beef is ready spread a thin layer of pasta sauce in the bottom of a baking dish
  5. Then add a layer of cooked lasagna sheet
  6. Then add a layer of the meat sauce we cooked before
  7. Spread a even layer of cheese
  8. Repeat those layers two times
